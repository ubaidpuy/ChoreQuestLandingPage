Simple Setup

Up and Running in 10 Minutes

Three steps. That's it. Then ChoreQuest runs on autopilot — no daily management needed.

  1. 1
    📝

    Set Up Quests

    Add chores, homework, or any task. Set the credit reward for each one. Dishes? 20 credits. Full bedroom clean? 30. Totally customizable.

  2. 2
    📷

    Kids Complete & Submit

    Your kid finishes the chore, snaps a photo to prove it's done. Credits appear in their balance instantly after your quick approval.

  3. 3
    🔒

    Screen Time Unlocks

    Credits automatically unlock apps, games, and YouTube through Apple Screen Time. No manual overrides. No fights. Just results.
